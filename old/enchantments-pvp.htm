Legend
AEP: Agility Equivalence Points. A method to convert the value of item attributes to compare them in terms of Agility points. Based on the forum discussion by Ming from Lightning's Blade.
AEP: PVP-Oriented formula which gives more weight to Stamina and defensive stats. MAEP: Maximum DPS AEP for use in maximizing PVE raid DPS. TAEP: Tank AEP used for avoidance tanking. P: Phase.
Arm: Armor, Agi: Agility, Sta: Stamina, Str: Strength, AP: Attack Power, Cri: Critical Strike %, Dod: Dodge %, Def: Defense, Resistances: Ar: Arcane, Fi: Fire, Fr: Frost, Na: Nature, Sh: Shadow, HP: Health.
PVP: PVP Special Value, PVE: PVE Special Value, Tank: Tank Special Value.
